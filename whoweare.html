Welcome to EnerGlobix
A leading and trusted global energy & commodity
company committed to bringing the world closer
together through reliable energy solutions.
Purpose and Values
Our purpose is to deliver energy that fuels progress and connectivity across the globe.
We believe in integrity, innovation, and sustainability.
These core values guide every decision we make, from our daily operations to our long-term strategies.
Vision and Mission
Our vision is to be the most trusted energy provider, known for our commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability.
Our mission is to lead the
energy sector by delivering
superior products and
services, fostering strong
relationships with our
stakeholders, and making
a positive impact on the
communities we serve.
Our Global Reach
With a network of partners and clients worldwide, we have the scale
and expertise to meet the energy needs of diverse markets. Our
global presence allows us to leverage local knowledge and insights
to deliver customized solutions that drive
growth and development.
EnerGlobix Story
EnerGlobix leadership has had extensive
experience since 2010 with importing diesel and petrol
in Central Asia. Recognizing the need for reliable energy,
we strategically expanded the company into upstream,
midstream, and downstream operations, encompassing
crude oil refining, fuel processing, blending, distribution,
and fertiliser supply and distribution.
Key achievements
Crude Oil Refining
Established advanced refining processes.
Fuel Processing and Blending
Developed sophisticated fuel processing facilities.
Supply & Distribution
Ensured efficient fuel and LPG distribution across Central Asia.
Quality Control
Implemented rigorous testing and laboratory services.
Exploration & Production
Expanded into discovering and extracting oil and gas resources.
