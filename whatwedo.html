Our Commodities
EnerGlobix provide a wide array of energy & commodity
products, each sourced to meet the highest standards
of performance, sustainability and reliability.
TC1 Aviation Fuel
TC1 Aviation Fuel is a high-performance fuel specifically designed for jet engines.
Used by commercial airlines, private jets, air cargo and military aircraft, TC1 Aviation Fuel is critical for reliable and efficient aviation operations worldwide.
This fuel ensures that millions of people can travel quickly and safely across the globe, connecting families, facilitating business, and supporting tourism.
Diesel
Our diesel fuel is formulated for transportation and industrial uses, providing reliable energy for vehicles and machinery. It offers high efficiency and reduced emissions, supporting both performance and environmental goals.
Our diesel fuel supports a wide range of transportation and heavy-duty applications.
Diesel powers the trucks that deliver goods, the buses that transport people, and the machinery that builds infrastructure, playing a crucial role in our daily logistics and construction activities.
Petrol
Petrol is a versatile fuel for internal combustion engines in vehicles and small machinery. Our petrol products ensure consistent performance and efficiency, making them a reliable choice for everyday use.
Used in cars, motorcycles and other small engines, our petrol fuels a broad spectrum of transportation and personal use needs.
Petrol fuels the vehicles that many of us use for our daily commutes and recreational activities, making it an essential part of everyday life.
Crude
Crude oil is the raw, unrefined petroleum extracted from the ground. It serves as the primary feedstock for refineries, where it is processed into various refined products such as petrol, diesel, and jet fuel.
Crude oil is fundamental to the global energy supply chain, feeding refineries that produce fuels, lubricants, and petrochemicals used across industries.
Crude oil is the starting point for many of the fuels and products we rely on every day, from the petrol in our cars to the plastics used in countless applications.
Coal
Coal is a fossil fuel used primarily for electricity generation and industrial processes. Our high-grade coal ensures efficient energy production with lower emissions, supporting sustainable energy goals.
Available in various grades to suit different industrial needs.
Coal has a significant role in electricity generation, providing power to homes and businesses, and is also crucial in producing materials like steel and cement used in construction.
Fertilizer
Fertilizers are essential agricultural products that enhance soil fertility and boost crop yields. Our fertilizers are designed to provide essential nutrients that promote healthy plant growth and increase agricultural productivity.
Widely used in farming, horticulture, and landscaping, our fertilizers support food production and sustainable agriculture practices.
Fertilizers are vital for growing the food that sustains us, ensuring that crops are healthy and productive, which in turn supports global food security.

agriculture, and manufacturing.
